About Spellsmith

Welcome to Spellsmith, a community-driven platform dedicated to exploring and expanding the magic of the upcoming DC20 Roleplaying System by Dungeon Coach. This website is a fan-built initiative designed to serve as a hub for homebrew spells, giving players and Game Masters alike a place to discover, share, and enhance their gameplay experience.

At Spellsmith, we aim to bring creativity and collaboration to the forefront. Our growing collection of homebrew spells is contributed by passionate members of the DC20 community. Whether you're looking for unique elemental attacks, creative utility spells, or game-changing enchantments, you'll find a wide range of magical possibilities here, all tailored to fit DC20's dynamic mechanics.
